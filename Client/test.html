About Configure your bot

We developed web scrappers that automate the housing search in the Netherlands for our personal use. Given the success we had, we decided to make the software public. Instead of just distributing it, we decided to take care of running it too and turn it into a subscription service. You choose the city in which you are looking for housing and receive the software's output via email.

The software searches Plaza, RoomPlaza, Huurwoningen and Kamernet each minute and outputs any new listings it finds. We run them for every city in the Netherlands, construct a tailored email with newly found listings and send it to you.

We charge 5 eur for 30 days of receiving alerts. This is a one-time payment and we will remind you when your bot is about to expire in case you wish extending. Check the 'Pricing' page where we break down exactly how your subscription works.

We want this tool to be as helpful as possible. If there is any way to make it better for you, please do not hesitate to mention it below. We will change it according to your needs.

No account needed. Input the email address you wish receiving the alerts at and setup your preferences for each website.

When you are ready, press 'Next'. After confirming your choices, you will be taken to the payment portal.

You can also press 'Check' to see if an active subscription with that email already exists.
